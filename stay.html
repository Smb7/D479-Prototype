Where to Stay

From an inexpensive hostel to a four-star resort — all lodging is regulated and inspected by the Tanitian government

Resort

Taniti's premier full-service luxury property.

4-Star Resort

Yellow Leaf Bay Resort

Taniti's one large, four-star resort offers full-service amenities including beachfront access on Yellow Leaf Bay, on-site dining, and a spa. The island's top luxury accommodation option, strictly regulated and inspected by the Tanitian government.

Hotels

A range of small, family-owned hotels spread across Taniti City and beyond.

Hotel

Taniti City Inn

A welcoming family-owned hotel situated in the heart of Taniti City, steps from native architecture, local shops, and the white sandy beaches of Yellow Leaf Bay.

Hotel

Merriton Landing Hotel

Located on the rapidly developing north side of Yellow Leaf Bay, this hotel puts you close to entertainment venues, dining, and easy walking access to local attractions.

Hotel

Harbor View Hotel

A comfortable family-run property overlooking the harbor. A great base for those arriving by cruise ship or exploring the island by rental car.

Bed & Breakfasts

A growing number of locally owned B&Bs offer an intimate, authentic Tanitian stay.

B&B

Island Garden B&B

One of Taniti's growing bed & breakfast options, set among tropical gardens with personalized hospitality. A homemade breakfast featuring local fish and fresh fruit is served each morning.

B&B

Yellow Leaf B&B

A cozy guesthouse within walking distance of Yellow Leaf Bay's white sandy beaches. Ideal for couples or solo travelers seeking a quiet, community-oriented base.

🏕️
Budget Option: Hostel

For the budget-conscious traveler, Taniti also has one inexpensive hostel conveniently located near Taniti City. All lodging on the island — including the hostel — is strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.
